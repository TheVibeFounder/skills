01Why this exists

Not inspired. Convinced
you can do it too.

Most AI content talks about what's possible. You read it, save it, go back to your day exactly the same. This is different. A Forbes 30U30 founder who scaled Simplified AI to 20M users — now building live on camera, showing the tools, the configs, the decisions, the mistakes.

What it is
A founder running real businesses with AI agents — building live so you can see exactly how it works.
What it is not
Not a course. Not a tutorial. Not "10 AI prompts." Real builds. Real numbers. Real process — including what broke.
The promise
Every Sunday, live on camera, unedited. Copy what applies. Leave the rest.

+ Layout spacing uses the 8pt scale. Optical micro-adjustments in typography only.
