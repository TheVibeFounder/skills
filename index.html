What I believe

Scale used to mean hiring. AI is compressing execution so fast that coordination is becoming the new tax. Teams that add headcount early will lose to smaller operators with sharper taste and faster loops.

The good news: when execution gets cheap, more people get to act on what they know — without building an institution around themselves first.
